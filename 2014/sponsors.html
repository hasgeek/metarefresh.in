MyParichay

“Parichay” in Sanskrit means providing a formal introduction of a trusted person. MyParichay is a dedicated group of successful entrepreneurs, HR professionals, and computer scientists that want to capture the spirit behind a Parichay (an introduction) from a known person to transform how people achieve their career potential and companies find the best quality talent.

Our next generation social recruitment solution is already helping a number of leading companies connect to talent not found through traditional recruitment channels.

Some of the interesting areas our engineering team works on are:

  • Data mining and Retrieval, Search, Recommendations
  • Platform interoperability with existing social, web and enterprise channels
  • Extended communication framework spanning across email, social and mobile channels
  • Scaling the platform to support growth of thousands of daily new candidates and recruiters while reducing latencies.
