Good Enough Design for Responsive Web Apps

One-day workshop on coordinating responsive web design on 10 September: Saneef Ansari and Brajeshwari Oinam will show you how to create a good (enough) system and custom front-end patterns for your web app that works across devices of any screen size, and speed up development by helping your team coordinate using a living style guide.

Good Enough Design for Responsive Web Apps

Brajeshwar Oinam and Saneef Ansari

Good Enough Design for Responsive Web Apps

Designing and developing responsive web is still a mystery for many team. Through this workshop you should be able to create a good enough design system and a custom front-end patterns for you web app that works across devices of any screensize - small (phones), medium (tablets), large (desktop) and more (TV). Through smart choices we can speed up the design phase and through a living style guide speed up the development. With the living style guide maintain and scale your web app with out breaking your teams brains. All this will help you to spend the energy – wasted on fights between your development and design teams – on productive things.
